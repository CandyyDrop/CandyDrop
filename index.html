Current Project

Spring Time Island

18+ ONLY

"It has to be me."

Kicked out by his strict Father, our Hero Mikey moves back in with a lovely mature lady and her two lovely daughters on Spring Time Island, his hometown. Back in his hometown, he joins the Spring Time Academy, which coincidentally is looking for a new Student Council President.

So Mikey thinks: 'It has to be me.' With the Summer break right around the corner, join School Clubs, befriend the female Students with your irresistible charm, and get your popularity up to become the Student Council President.
