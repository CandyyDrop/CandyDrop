Welcome to CandyDrop

“Your sweet escape to paradise!”

CandyDrop isn’t a massive studio; it’s a solo passion project built around vibrant storytelling, unexpected living situations, and the joy of bringing deliciously fun characters to life.

My current project, Spring Time Island, is the beating heart of CandyDrop. It’s an ever-expanding tropical adventure packed with school politics and romance, where I pour my imagination and love for visual novels into every breezy update.

At CandyDrop, the motto is simple: “Your sweet escape to paradise!”

Current Project

Spring Time Island

18+ ONLY

Kicked out by your strict father, you're forced to pack your bags and move to a tropical paradise—specifically, into the home of your father's beautiful ex-wife and her two lovely daughters. To make matters even more complicated, her sister and mother live right next door!

But you aren't just there to lounge on the beach. Your ultimate goal? To rule the school as its next President and build the ultimate island harem. To secure the votes you need, you'll have to explore the city, join multiple clubs, and charm as many friends (and lovers) as possible. Popularity is power on Spring Time Island.
